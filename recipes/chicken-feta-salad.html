Description

A tasty and protein-packed salad.

Ingredients

  • 169g Chicken thigh
  • 52g Lettuce
  • 63g Cucumber
  • 63g Tomato
  • 38g Red Onion
  • 50g Feta cheese
  • 38g 26g Olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash and prep the salad.
  2. Use lettuce as the base then top with sliced cucumber, onion and tomato.
  3. Pan-fry the slice chicken breast with herbs until cooked through.
  4. Top salad with chicken and then drizzle olive oil as the dressing. Amazing.
  5. Tip: If you are short for time you can replace fresh chickenwith pre-cooked deli Tegalroast chicken meat. Optional Seasoning - sprinkle over some fresh herbs or lime juice to serve.
