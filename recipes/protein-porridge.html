Description

Protein filled porridge, with frozen blueberries and peanut butter

Ingredients

  • 70g rolled oats
  • 26g protein powder
  • 290g almond milk (unsweetened)
  • 174g mixed berries
  • 18g natural peanut utter

Steps

  1. Mix oats with unsweetened almond milk (add more water if needed to get to your desired consistency) and microwave for 1 min.
  2. Add protein powder and natural peanut butter and mix in well.
  3. Microwave for another 90 sec or until at desired consistency.
  4. Mix in berries and serve. Easy but delicious.
  5. Tip: If you have time cook on stove and bring oats to boil then add protein powder and natural peanut butter and simmer till cooked then add mixed berries.
  6. Optional Seasoning - simply sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon on top to spice up your brekkie
